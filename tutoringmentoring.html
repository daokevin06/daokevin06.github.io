Mentoring

I often mentor undergraduates through UW-Madison's Directed Reading Program. If you are an undergraduate interested in representation theory or algebraic geometry, and want to do a directed reading, then feel free to reach out! Some past topics of DRPs I have mentored for are the following: Atiyah-Macdonald's Introduction to Commutative Algebra, Miranda's book on Algebraic Curves and Riemann Surfaces, Cox-Little-O'Shea's book on Ideals, Varieties, and Algorithms, Stein and Shakarchi's book on Complex Analysis, and Serre's book on the representation theory of finite groups.

Tutoring

I have experience tutoring students anywhere taking classes anywhere from the middle school level to the upper division pure mathematics level. If you are specifically seeking me out as a tutor, then feel free to reach out and we might be able to work something out!
